7. Appendix B: VAB-1000-L Daughter Board (optional)¶

7.1. Specifications¶

  • PCB: 68 mm x 47 mm (L x W), 4-Layer
  • Onboard Connectors
    • 1 x Board-To-Board connector
    • 2 x LVDS connectors
    • 2 x Backlight connectors
    • 1 x DC-In connector
  • Onboard Jumper
    • 1 x BL_SET

Note

Please ensure that all items in the packing list are present before using this product. If any of the items are missing or damaged, contact your distributor or sales representative immediately.
